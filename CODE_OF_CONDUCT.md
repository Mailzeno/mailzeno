# Code of Conduct

MailZeno is committed to fostering a welcoming and respectful community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Public or private abuse

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions
that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

By participating, you agree to uphold these standards.
